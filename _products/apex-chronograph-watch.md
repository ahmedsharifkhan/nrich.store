Built for performance and precision. The Apex Chronograph combines a sporty aesthetic with a high-precision quartz movement and luminous hands for low-light readability.

The ion-plated case gives a stealthy, all-black appearance with subtle orange accents on the subdials and bezel. Three-eye chronograph design with pushers at 2 and 4 o'clock, seconds subdial at 9, and date window at 3. The silicone band ensures comfort during intense activity.
