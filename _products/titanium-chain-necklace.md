A minimalist Cuban link chain machined from grade-5 titanium — hypoallergenic, lightweight, and built to last. Wear solo or layered for a bold statement.

Grade-5 titanium (also known as Ti-6Al-4V) is the same alloy used in aerospace and medical applications. It is stronger than steel at a fraction of the weight, completely hypoallergenic, and will never tarnish or discolour. Available in three lengths and two finishes to suit every style.
