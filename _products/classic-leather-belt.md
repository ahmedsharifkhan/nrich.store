A heritage leather belt that only gets better with age. Full-grain vegetable-tanned leather, hand-burnished edges, and a solid brass pin buckle.

Vegetable tanning is a centuries-old craft that uses natural tree bark extracts rather than chemicals. The resulting leather is firmer, develops a beautiful patina with wear, and is more environmentally responsible. Each belt is cut from a single piece of full-grain leather — the most durable and premium grade available — with no splits or corrections.
